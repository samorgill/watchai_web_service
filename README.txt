Watchai Web Service

Deployed to Google App Engine at http://3-dot-projectbabywatch.appspot.com

Takes information from HTML form, uses JavaScript/Ajax/JQuery to perform HTTP calls and Servlets/ web.xml mapping to make the request/responses to the web service.

Navigate to http://projectbabywatch.appspot.com
Register your account by filling in your username, password, email address, phone number and Hub ID (an 11 digit IP address found on the home screen of your smartphone app).
The things registered to your account will automatically appear with their current on/off or locked/unlocked state, click on them to change the state of a thing
To add a thing to your account enter the details of the sensor or actuator in the Add section
